Studio S Dance Company

Studio S Dance Company has been running for over 3 years and provides dance classes in Rochford, in contemporary commercial, technique, and more. For ages 5-18 years of age. Our dance classes are taught by experienced, trained teachers who provide knowledge, skill and passion. Fun classes for all ages whether your just starting out or an experienced dancer there is a class to suit all. At Studio S we have a dance competition team that train and compete in competitions around the UK, a great way to continuously train and perform. We put on two dance show every a year, one at Christmas and one in the summer, offering students the opportunity to perform on stage. We have an exam term where students are examined on their progress and dance skills, these are optional for all to take part in. Come and join our dance family.
